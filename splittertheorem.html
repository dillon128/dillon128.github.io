Splitter Theorem resources

I have been working with Carolyn Chun and James Oxley to obtain a splitter theorem for internally 4-connected binary matroids. This page contains some resources associated with one step in the proof of the theorem.

  • BinarySplitter.sws: The Sage notebook containing the code required for the case analysis.
  • catalogue.sobj: The catalogue of 3-connected binary matroids with at most 15 elements and rank at most 7.
  • ifccatalogue.sobj: The catalogue of internally 4-connected binary matroids with at most 15 elements and rank at most 7.
  • targetminors.sobj: The index showing which internally 4-connected binary matroids contain specified 10- or 11-element matroids as minors.
